Welcome to Wikipedia

Wikipedia is a free online encyclopedia that anyone can edit. It is written collaboratively by volunteers around the world and hosted by the Wikimedia Foundation. Wikipedia contains millions of articles covering a wide range of topics including science, history, geography, technology, culture, and current events.

The goal of Wikipedia is to provide reliable and neutral information to people everywhere, free of charge. Anyone with internet access can contribute by improving existing articles or creating new ones.

Featured Article

Earth is the third planet from the Sun and the only astronomical object known to support life. About 71% of Earth's surface is covered with water, and its atmosphere protects life from harmful solar radiation.

Earth formed approximately 4.5 billion years ago and has diverse climates, ecosystems, and geological features.
